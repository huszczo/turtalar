WHY Tultalar® IS THE BEST CHOICE

  • Low Pricing: We work hard to provide some of the lowest prices for printing in the industry, especially for large-format 3D printing. We want you to be able to afford to iterate on your design and produce multiple prototypes of your project. Check out our pricing page for more information.
  • Rapid Turnaround: We’re the fastest 3D printing service you’ll find. In many cases, we can have prints ready by the next day. You chose 3D printing because of its rapid prototyping capabilities, and we’ll make sure you get your parts quickly so you can move forward with your project.
  • Personal Service: We’re real people, not a faceless 3D printing service, and we personally handle every 3D printing order. You’ll be working directly with someone who has thousands of hours of 3D printing experience.

PROTOTYPING SERVICES

Working on a new design? Here’s how we can help:

  • Test-Fit Service We can 3D print small parts of your design and test-fit them together and suggest tweaks to improve the fit. This saves you time and money, allowing you to only commit to a full 3D print once you’re confident the parts will fit together the way you expect.
  • 3D Printing Consulting: Not sure what settings you need for your 3D prints, or which material to use? We’re here to help. We can advise you on the best settings and material for your application.
  • Design Services: Need help with the 3D design itself? We offer a 3D design service to help you bring your ideas to life.
